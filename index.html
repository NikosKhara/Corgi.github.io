About

Corgis originate from Pembroke, Welsh.

Corgi’s are in the herding group of dogs, are 10 - 12 inches tall, up to 30Lb, and live 12 - 13 years.

They’re pretty good with kids but can get really excited so its worth keeping an eye on them sometimes, same thing when they’re with other dogs.

They’re pretty easy to train but are also really smart so they can disobey you on purpose.

They have a pretty long coat and shed an insane amount, especially during spring and autumn which is there shedding season.

They also love to be loud and bark, have a ton of energy, love to play whenever possible, and are really hard to buy as puppy's.

Before Buying

Corgis are very energetic and require a lot of work. However that makes them great for learning how to be responsible for something.

They are awesome for your first dog, very sweet, and smart.

However if you want your house to be clean you should also brush them at least once a week and vacuum a lot.

They need to be played with/ran around with at least once a day.

You should feed them 2 - 3 times a day with a quarter cup of dog food. (Note - They will beg for more)
